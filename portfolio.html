About Me

Shubhangee Goel is a distinguished Kathak exponent, teacher, and performer, specializing in the prestigious Lucknow Gharana style. Her dedication to the classical arts has earned her numerous accolades, including the National Balshree Award and the IAYP award by the Duke of Edinburgh.

Her performances masterfully showcase the intricate dancing techniques of Lucknow Gharana in Kathak, particularly through her mesmerizing Tarana presentations. As a dedicated teacher, she continues to pass on these rich traditions to the next generation of dancers.
